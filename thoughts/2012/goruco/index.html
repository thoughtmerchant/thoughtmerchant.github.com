GORUCO 2012

Organizer and designer for the premier Ruby conference in New York City.

Each year I have been in NYC I have helped organize GORUCO Ruby conference. 2012 was a really special year for us since we changed venues and doubled the size of the conference. We bring over 250 people from all over the United States and abroad to hear talks from the most talented Rubyists in the world.
