About Thought Merchants

I make stuff, it just happens to take place on the web most of the time. Sometimes I start off creating someone’s brand, other times I create an online experience, or make a web application more usable.

I love being able to help improve business’ processes, and improve what they offer (or their perception). Bottom line is that I want to have fun and make what you have better. You could say I make things “good”. With the right client I can make it great.
