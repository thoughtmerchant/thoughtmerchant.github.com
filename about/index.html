About Thought Merchants

I specialize in creating usable experiences. I have created experiences companies such as Urban Outfitters, QVC, GSI Commerce, and Groupon currently use. I have also consulted for start-ups, and emerging companies like: Efficiency 2.0, Monetate, Hoopla, and FizzLaw.

My experience encompasses creating experiences online, and offline for products and services for both very broad B2C experiences, as well as complex domain specific B2B business applications.

You can email me at: steve@thoughtmerchants.com and follow me here: @thoughtmerchant
